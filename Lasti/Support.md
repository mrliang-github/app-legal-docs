## English Version

### Getting Started

Welcome to Lasti! This guide will help you get the most out of the app.

#### First Launch
When you first open Lasti, you'll see a sample event "💇 Haircut" with a record from 30 days ago. This helps you understand how the app works. You can delete this sample event anytime by swiping left on it.

#### Creating Your First Event
1. Tap the **+** button in the top right corner
2. Enter an event name (e.g., "Change Bedding")
3. Select an emoji icon from the preset options
4. Choose a color theme
5. Tap **Save**

#### Adding a Widget
1. Long press on your iPhone home screen
2. Tap the **+** button in the top left
3. Search for "Lasti"
4. Select the medium-sized widget
5. Tap **Add Widget**
6. Tap **Done**

### Frequently Asked Questions

#### General Questions

**Q: How many events can I create?**

A: The free version supports up to 6 events. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited events.

**Q: Is my data synced to the cloud?**

A: No. Lasti is designed with a "Local First" philosophy. All your data is stored only on your device. We recommend using the export feature regularly to back up your data.

**Q: How do I back up my data?**

A: Go to **Settings > Export Data**. Your data will be exported as a JSON file that you can save to Files, email to yourself, or store in your preferred cloud service.

**Q: What happens to my data if I delete the app?**

A: All data will be permanently deleted. Please export your data before uninstalling if you want to keep it.

---

#### Widget Questions

**Q: How does the widget work?**

A: The widget shows your first 4 events. Tap any event to:
- If not recorded today → Record today's date
- If already recorded today → Undo the record

**Q: Why isn't my widget updating?**

A: Try these solutions:
1. Remove and re-add the widget
2. Open the Lasti app to trigger a refresh
3. Restart your iPhone

**Q: Can I customize which events appear in the widget?**

A: The widget displays your first 4 events based on their sort order. To change which events appear, reorder your events in the app by long-pressing and dragging them.

---

#### Subscription Questions

**Q: What's included in Premium?**

A: Premium unlocks unlimited events. All other features (widget, calendar view, data export) are available in the free version.

**Q: How do I restore my purchase?**

A: Go to **Settings > Restore Purchases**. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

**Q: How do I cancel my subscription?**

A:
1. Open **Settings** app on your iPhone
2. Tap your **Apple ID** at the top
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Find **Lasti** and tap it
5. Tap **Cancel Subscription**

**Q: Can I get a refund?**

A: Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com) to request a refund.

---

#### Troubleshooting

**Q: The app is crashing. What should I do?**

A: Try these steps:
1. Close and reopen the app
2. Restart your iPhone
3. Update to the latest version of Lasti
4. If the problem persists, contact us at mrliang8816@gmail.com

**Q: I accidentally deleted an event. Can I recover it?**

A: Unfortunately, deleted events cannot be recovered. This is why we recommend regularly exporting your data as a backup.

**Q: My records are showing the wrong date. Why?**

A: Lasti uses your device's local time zone. Make sure your device's date and time settings are correct:
1. Open **Settings**
2. Go to **General > Date & Time**
3. Enable **Set Automatically**

### 常见问题

#### 一般问题

**问：我可以创建多少个事件？**

答：免费版支持最多 6 个事件。升级到高级版可解锁无限事件。

**问：我的数据会同步到云端吗？**

答：不会。Lasti 采用"本地优先"的设计理念。您的所有数据仅存储在您的设备上。我们建议定期使用导出功能备份您的数据。

**问：如何备份我的数据？**

答：前往 **设置 > 导出数据**。您的数据将导出为 JSON 文件，您可以保存到"文件"应用、发送到邮箱或存储到您首选的云服务。

**问：如果我删除应用，我的数据会怎样？**

答：所有数据将被永久删除。如果您想保留数据，请在卸载前导出。

---

#### 小组件问题

**问：小组件是如何工作的？**

答：小组件显示您的前 4 个事件。点击任意事件：
- 如果今天没有记录 → 记录今天的日期
- 如果今天已经记录 → 撤销记录

**问：为什么我的小组件没有更新？**

答：尝试以下解决方案：
1. 移除并重新添加小组件
2. 打开 Lasti 应用以触发刷新
3. 重启您的 iPhone

**问：我可以自定义小组件中显示哪些事件吗？**

答：小组件根据排序顺序显示您的前 4 个事件。要更改显示的事件，请在应用中长按并拖动来重新排序您的事件。

---

#### 订阅问题

**问：高级版包含什么？**

答：高级版解锁无限事件。所有其他功能（小组件、日历视图、数据导出）在免费版中均可使用。

**问：如何恢复我的购买？**

答：前往 **设置 > 恢复购买**。确保您使用原始购买时相同的 Apple ID 登录。

**问：如何取消订阅？**

答：
1. 打开 iPhone 上的 **设置** 应用
2. 点击顶部的 **Apple ID**
3. 点击 **订阅**
4. 找到 **Lasti** 并点击
5. 点击 **取消订阅**

**问：我可以获得退款吗？**

答：退款由 Apple 处理。访问 [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com) 申请退款。

#### 故障排除

**问：应用崩溃了，我该怎么办？**

答：尝试以下步骤：
1. 关闭并重新打开应用
2. 重启您的 iPhone
3. 更新到最新版本的 Lasti
4. 如果问题仍然存在，请通过 mrliang8816@gmail.com 联系我们

**问：我不小心删除了一个事件。可以恢复吗？**

答：很遗憾，已删除的事件无法恢复。这就是我们建议定期导出数据作为备份的原因。

**问：我的记录显示的日期不对，为什么？**

答：Lasti 使用您设备的本地时区。请确保您设备的日期和时间设置正确：
1. 打开 **设置**
2. 前往 **通用 > 日期与时间**
3. 启用 **自动设置**
