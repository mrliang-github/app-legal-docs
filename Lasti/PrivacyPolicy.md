**Last Updated / 最后更新**: December 26, 2025

## English Version

### Introduction

Lasti ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

### Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

Lasti is designed with a "Local First" philosophy. All your data is stored exclusively on your device:

- **Event Data**: Names, icons, and colors of events you create
- **Record Data**: Timestamps and optional notes for your records
- **App Settings**: Your preferences and configurations

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

### Data Storage

- All data is stored locally on your iOS device using Apple's SwiftData framework
- Data is stored in your device's secure container
- We do not have access to your data
- We do not use cloud servers or databases

### Data Export

You can export your data at any time through the Settings menu. The exported data is in JSON format and is shared using Apple's standard Share Sheet. We do not receive or store any exported data.

### Third-Party Services

**StoreKit (Apple)**

We use Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases and subscriptions. Apple may collect transaction data according to their privacy policy. Please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/) for more information.

### Analytics

We do not use any analytics or tracking services. We do not collect:

- Usage statistics
- Device information
- Location data
- Personal identifiers

### Children's Privacy

Lasti does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age. The App is designed for general audiences.

### Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, data security is managed by iOS's built-in security features, including:

- Device passcode protection
- Face ID / Touch ID
- Device encryption

### Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access**: View all your data within the App
- **Export**: Export your data in JSON format anytime
- **Delete**: Delete individual records, events, or all data
- **Portability**: Your exported data is in a standard, readable format

### Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

## 中文版本

### 简介

Lasti（以下简称"我们"或"本应用"）致力于保护您的隐私。本隐私政策说明了当您使用我们的移动应用程序时，我们如何处理您的信息。

### 我们收集的信息

**我们不收集任何个人信息。**

Lasti 采用"本地优先"的设计理念。您的所有数据都仅存储在您的设备上：

- **事件数据**：您创建的事件名称、图标和颜色
- **记录数据**：您记录的时间戳和可选备注
- **应用设置**：您的偏好和配置

除非您明确选择导出，否则这些数据永远不会离开您的设备。

### 数据存储

- 所有数据都使用 Apple 的 SwiftData 框架本地存储在您的 iOS 设备上
- 数据存储在您设备的安全容器中
- 我们无法访问您的数据
- 我们不使用云服务器或数据库

### 数据导出

您可以随时通过设置菜单导出您的数据。导出的数据为 JSON 格式，通过 Apple 标准的分享面板进行分享。我们不会接收或存储任何导出的数据。

### 第三方服务

**StoreKit (Apple)**

我们使用 Apple 的 StoreKit 进行应用内购买和订阅。Apple 可能会根据其隐私政策收集交易数据。请参阅 [Apple 隐私政策](https://www.apple.com/cn/privacy/) 了解更多信息。

### 分析工具

我们不使用任何分析或追踪服务。我们不收集：

- 使用统计数据
- 设备信息
- 位置数据
- 个人标识符

### 儿童隐私

Lasti 不会故意收集 13 岁以下儿童的任何信息。本应用面向一般受众设计。

### 数据安全

由于所有数据都存储在您的设备本地，数据安全由 iOS 内置的安全功能管理，包括：

- 设备密码保护
- 面容 ID / 触控 ID
- 设备加密

### 您的权利

您对数据拥有完全控制权：

- **访问**：在应用内查看所有数据
- **导出**：随时以 JSON 格式导出数据
- **删除**：删除单条记录、事件或所有数据
- **可移植性**：导出的数据采用标准可读格式

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。如有任何更改，我们将更新"最后更新"日期以通知您。
